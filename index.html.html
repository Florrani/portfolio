About Me...

My name is Florrani Kaye Managing. I am a Filipino- Australian visual designer. I am currently in my second year of university, studying Visual Communication Design at Queensland University of Technology (QUT) in Brisbane, Australia. I am also taking units in UI/UX Design and preproduction animation, in order to expand my skills on other design disciplines. I am a designer who is committed to creating purposeful visual designs for people. I strive to spread creativity while also developing the best possible outcome in all my projects.
